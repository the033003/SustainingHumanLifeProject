Tracking a Month of Sustainable Living

Reducing food waste to spend less.

Project Overview

This project tracks daily food purchases, consumption, and waste over one month to identify patterns, reduce waste, and optimize budget.

Key goals:

  • Minimize food waste through better planning
  • Optimize grocery spending without sacrificing nutrition
  • Promote sustainable and mindful consumption habits
